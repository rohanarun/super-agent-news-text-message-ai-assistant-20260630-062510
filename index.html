How a text turns into real work

1. You text the request

Natural SMS-style messages like “remind me to rebook my flight next week” or “check my calendar and draft an email.”

2. Super plans and acts

Super opens browsers, logs in where needed, and executes multi-step workflows on a real computer — with you approving sensitive steps.

3. Memory compounds

Completed steps are stored in a reusable computer-use cache, so similar future tasks don’t start from zero.

Why text-based assistants are having a moment

Recent launches show renewed interest in SMS and notification-based assistants, from dedicated SMS bots to mainstream platforms extending assistants into messaging surfaces (The AI Journal).

At the same time, major assistants like Siri and Gemini are becoming more agentic, highlighting demand for assistants that can actually do things, not just reply (Apple), (blog.google).
